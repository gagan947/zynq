Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Zynq Nordic AB (“ZYNQ”, “we”, “us”) processes personal data when you use our app, website, and related services (“Services”). It is separate from our Terms & Conditions and covers data protection only.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account & contact details: name, email, phone, login credentials.
  • Skin & analysis data: photos, videos, and other inputs for AI analysis. These are deleted within seconds unless you choose to save them in your profile.
  • Booking details: selected clinic, time, treatment, communication via chat/video.
  • Payment details: processed by Swish, Klarna, or Stripe. ZYNQ never stores full card/bank details.
  • Technical information: IP address, device data, logs, cookies.

2. How We Use Data

We process data to:

  • Provide AI-powered skin analysis.
  • Enable bookings and consultations with clinics.
  • Display relevant offers and sponsored content (always clearly marked).
  • Improve app functionality and comply with legal requirements (e.g., accounting).

3. Our Role vs. Clinics’ Role

  • ZYNQ acts as a platform intermediary.
  • We facilitate bookings and communication.
  • Clinics are data controllers for care/treatment data.
  • ZYNQ is data controller for account, AI analysis, and technical data.

4. Legal Basis

  • Consent – for sensitive personal data (skin & health data).
  • Contract – to process bookings and communication.
  • Legal obligation – accounting, GDPR.
  • Legitimate interest – security, support, improvements.

5. Data Sharing

We only share data when necessary:

  • Clinics access booking and analysis data for consultation/treatment.
  • Payment partners: Swish, Klarna, Stripe handle payment transactions.
  • Technical suppliers: hosting, support, infrastructure.
  • Authorities: when legally required.

We never sell your personal data.

6. Retention & Deletion

  • Selfies/analysis data deleted within seconds unless saved.
  • Saved profile data deleted when you close your account.
  • Booking/payment data stored for 7 years (legal requirement).

7. Your GDPR Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict processing.
  • Request data portability.
  • File complaints with the relevant supervisory authority.

8. Under 18

ZYNQ is not intended for users under 18. If we learn such data has been collected, it will be deleted without undue delay.

9. Security & Incident Handling

We apply encryption, firewalls, and access controls. All data is stored within the EU/EEA or under equivalent safeguards (e.g., EU Standard Contractual Clauses). In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify the supervisory authority within 72 hours and affected users without undue delay.

10. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

ZYNQ has not appointed a Data Protection Officer at this time. If a DPO is appointed in the future, this policy will be updated with contact details.

11. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via app or email.
