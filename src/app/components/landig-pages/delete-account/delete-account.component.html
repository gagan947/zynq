Delete Account

Steps to Delete Your Account
  1. Log in with your credentials.
  2. Tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar (right side).
  3. Tap the settings icon in the top right corner.
  4. Scroll down and find the "Delete Account" option (below "Logout").
  5. Tap on "Delete Account" — a confirmation popup will appear.
  6. Confirm by tapping "Yes" — your account will be deleted.
Note: Once deleted, your account will be immediately and permanently removed.
